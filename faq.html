Are you official?

No.

Then who are you?

I am a PhD student at the University of Toronto. I had been collecting Canadian case data in a spreadsheet but soon this became infeasible. There is also no single source to get a good idea of the national cases, testing and hospitalization rates with provincial breakdowns and with the latest data. So, I built a tool to collect data from official provincial links and reports, and this website to provide the most up-to-date and clear picture of the pandemic with the focus on Canada.
Github

Why do you have more total cases than canada.ca?

The Public Health Agency of Canada updates the numbers at scheduled times. I collect data directly from provincial health authorities and reports which are most up-to-date.
